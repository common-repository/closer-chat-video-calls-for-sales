== Description ==

Closer - a conversational business platform that’s much more than a chat. Combining ultra-fast messaging with screen sharing, co-browsing, HD audio & video calls and bots allows you to move from lead capture widgets to a complete deal closer solution. **A free plan is available!**

= What do you get with Closer =
* Ultra-fast chat. Interact with your customers using text, images, emojis and more
* HD video & audio calls with customers while they’re on your website. Give your customers the best experience possible with high-definition audio and video conversations from any browser or mobile device
* A dedicated presentation mode. Easily share your screen with customers
* Online meetings scheduling with your leads. Book your demos with a few taps and effortlessly convert leads to customers
* Unlimited file uploads. Share and receive photos, videos, and any other documents with your customers
* Customers can switch between desktop and mobile. Cross-device continuity for your customers – they can switch between devices having the same conversations
* Contact history in a unified communication stream. See the details of every customer interaction at a second’s notice.
 
Closer helps your team close more deals from the first minutes, and they will love it. 

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/plugin-name` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
1. Open the “Closer Settings” screen in the main menu to configure the plugin
1. Enter the Company ID found in your Closer Dashboard -> Profile & Settings -> Widget configuration. If you don’t have a Closer account yet, create one for free at https://closer.app 


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How many users I can have on a free plan? =
There’s no limit of users you can add. Feel free to add as many team members as you need.

= Can I change widget color? =
Yes, you can change it to colors you want.

= How many contacts I can have on a free plan? =
There’s no limit of contacts you can have in Closer. Have as many conversations with your customers as you need.

= Can I see the history of past conversations? =
Yes, you have a history of each conversation inside adviser’s dashboard. 

= Can I have a conversation with website visitors while I’m only available on mobile? =
Yes, you can use Closer mobile app for you and your team to have a conversations with your customers when you’re on the go.

= What website visitors need to have a video or audio call with me? =
No! All they need is to have access to the internet.
